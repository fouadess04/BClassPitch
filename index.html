02 · The Pitch in 60 Seconds

Executive Summary

Elevator Pitch

The Problem

Pharmacy students across Algeria struggle with fragmented learning resources — scattered group chats, no trusted exam-adapted materials, and no access to past exam questions. General AI tools waste their time with irrelevant answers, while faculty restrictions block proper exam prep.

The Solution

BClass is an all-in-one learning platform. Our AI engine, B-AI, generates faculty-specific MCQs from student documents and past exams, with explanations sourced directly from course materials. BNotes — AI-generated summaries — tailor content to each faculty's exam patterns.

The Impact

Students study smarter, not harder. BClass consolidates assets, quizzes, summaries & past exams in one place — eliminating wasted time across every Algerian pharmacy faculty.

05 · How BClass Fixes It

The Solution

3-Step Workflow

Step 1 — Centralized Resource Hub

Promo delegates upload slides, PDFs & recordings. Everything is organized in one minimalist UI — instantly accessible faculty-wide, with cross-faculty discovery built in.

Step 2 — AI-Powered MCQ Generation

B-AI analyzes uploaded materials and the faculty's historical exams to auto-generate faculty-specific MCQs, with explanations cited straight from the source documents.

Step 3 — Exam-Aware Study Summaries

BNotes synthesize documents and exam patterns into focused summaries — teaching only what each faculty actually tests.

First of its kind in Algeria — faculty-specific exam alignment, not generic AI summaries like NotebookLM. Result: prep time drops from 4+ hours to under 1 hour per topic.
